## What I Do

I help people recognise and respond to cyber threats — through 
training, not just tools.

Most organisations have firewalls. Very few have staff who can 
spot a phishing email, question a suspicious request, or 
understand why their password matters. That gap is where I work.

My background is unusual for this field: I hold a Higher 
Certificate in Cybersecurity, I'm completing a BSc in Security 
& Network Engineering, and I've spent six years teaching Muay 
Thai — which means I know how to break down complex, high-stakes 
skills for people who are learning under pressure. I also run a 
small business (Ameer Fragrances), which means I understand the 
real-world operational context that most security awareness 
content ignores.

I'm building toward delivering security awareness training for 
SMEs and eventually founding a security consultancy. Right now 
I'm actively documenting my home lab, building my first pilot 
workshop, and pursuing Security+ certification.
